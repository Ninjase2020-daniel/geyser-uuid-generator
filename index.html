HOW FLOODGATE UUIDS WORK

Geyser's Floodgate plugin assigns every Bedrock Edition player a deterministic UUID based on their XUID (Xbox User ID). The UUID always starts with 00000000-0000-0000- and the remaining 16 hex characters are the XUID encoded as a big-endian 64-bit integer.

UUID FORMAT
00000000-0000-0000-HHHH-HHHHHHHHHHHH
│                   └── XUID as 16-char hex (big-endian)
└── always zero prefix (marks as Bedrock player)
EXAMPLE
XUID:  2535416409498934
  hex: 0009011234567890 (16 chars)
UUID: 00000000-0000-0000-0009-011234567890
